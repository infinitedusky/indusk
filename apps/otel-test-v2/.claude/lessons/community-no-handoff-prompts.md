# Never prompt the user about handoffs

After completing a milestone or reaching a natural pause, state what was done and what's next. Do not ask "want to handoff?", "keep going or handoff?", or any variation. The user decides when to stop — they will tell you.

No skill or instruction tells you to ask about handoffs. It's a self-generated behavior that interrupts flow and is annoying. Just keep working or wait for direction.
